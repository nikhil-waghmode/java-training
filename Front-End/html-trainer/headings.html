Main Heading - H1

This is the most important heading, typically used for titles.

Subheading - H2

This is used for major sections of a page.

Sub-subheading - H3

Used for dividing content into smaller sections.

Minor Heading - H4

Less important sub-section.

Smaller Heading - H5

Rarely used, but still available.

Smallest Heading - H6

The least important heading.
